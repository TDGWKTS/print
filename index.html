📄 Print Inspection Records

Select station and date to export inspection data
1
Download Inspection Data
Click Download Inspection Data to get the inspection records for the selected station and date.
2
Open Your Excel Template
Open your existing Excel template for the selected station.
3
Open Downloaded File
Open the downloaded Excel file.
4
Copy Data
Select all data cells from A2 to the last row/column, then press Ctrl+C.
5
Paste to Print Worksheet
In your template, go to the Print worksheet, select cell A6, then press Ctrl+V.
✓
Print
Your data is now in the Print sheet. Review and print.
💡 Important: Your Excel template is never modified.
Copy data from the downloaded file and paste into your Print sheet row 6.
All your formatting remains intact.
